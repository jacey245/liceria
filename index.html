About Liceria

Welcome to Liceria Kids Playground, where we've been creating magical childhood memories since 2015. Our state-of-the-art facility spans over 10,000 square feet of pure fun and excitement!

We believe that play is essential for child development. That's why we've carefully designed every corner of our playground to promote physical activity, creativity, and social interaction in a safe and supervised environment.
